About Us

We've Got One Mission: Getting the audience acquainted with cryptocurrencies

Since the invention of the Bitcoin(BTC), The Cryptocurrency Conference (formerly known as the TCC Conference) is a yearly international conference on the topic of cryptocurrencies. The Conference assembles investors, researchers, policymakers, practitioners, and end-users with one unifying goal: to envision and create the future of the cryptocurrencies.

We are happy to announce that The Cryptocurrency Conference will reconvene in London, the Heart of England, in August (1-3) 2021.
We've poured our energy into building a Conference that can help you continue to deliver important decisions in extraordinary times. The Cryptocurrency Conference will take place in London, England limited to 500 Attendees with several rooms which will be available during the conference including the main room, networking and break rooms, and Q&A rooms.

Our Objectives

  • We will work towards fair representation of all groups within Conference programmes, benchmarking ourselves against the diverse make-up of the UK population.
  • We will commit to working with our partners to help them encourage wider groups of individuals to speak and participate at the Conference.
  • We will make changes to our communications and physical environments to aim to make them accessible to as many groups of individuals as possible.
  • We will remain transparent about our progress and encourage our audience, speakers and partners to further challenge us.
