What is the price of the Pass?
A Pass for the Conference, taking place on 1-3 August, costs £20+VAT. Or book as a group of 5+ for an even better rate! Simply select 5 or more Passes and you'll only pay £15+VAT per pass.
Can I register multiple people at once?
Yes. You will be able to purchase multiple Conference Passes in one transaction. A group rate of £15+VAT per pass is available when you book 5 or more passes within one order. Get in touch if you're interested in registering a large group to find out more.
I am having trouble registering - who can I contact?
If you are experiencing difficulties with the registration form, please contact the Conference team by clicking here.
When and where does the Festival take place?
The Cryptocurrency Conference will take place online on 1-3 August 2021. Get a Virtual Pass to access to the Conference live in August and gain access to the sessions on demand for a period of 30 days once the virtual event has commenced.
